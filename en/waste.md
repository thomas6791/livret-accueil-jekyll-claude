## Recyclable Waste
Everything that is recyclable should go in the **yellow recycling bin** located downstairs in the courtyard next to the entrance.

This includes: cardboard, plastic packaging, metal, and paper. Even if they are dirty, they go in the yellow bin.

## Food Waste
Food waste goes in one of the containers in the street. There is one near the bird park.

## Glass and glass jars
Glass and glass jars go in the green containers.

## Other Waste
All other waste goes in the **blue bin** downstairs in the courtyard near the entrance. They must be in a plastic bag. The trash bin under the sink goes in the blue trash.
